Frequently Asked Questions About Fade

How much does a fade haircut cost at B & H Barber Shop?

We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments like Venmo and CashApp. First-time clients should note that complex designs or extra-long hair may incur additional charges, which we'll discuss during consultation.
Do I need an appointment or do you take walk-ins?
We accept both appointments and walk-ins at our East Village location. However, we strongly recommend booking appointments, especially during peak hours (weekday evenings and weekends). You can book online through our website or call us at (212) XXX-XXXX. Walk-in wait times typically range from 15-45 minutes depending on the day and time.
How long does a fade haircut typically take?
A standard fade haircut typically takes 30-35 minutes. Skin fades or more complex styles may take 45-50 minutes. If you're adding a beard trim or design work, please allow for up to an hour. We take our time to ensure precision and quality, especially around the fade progression and finishing details.
What should I bring or know before getting a fade at B & H?
It's helpful to bring reference photos of the fade style you want. Your hair should be clean and free of heavy products. If you're getting a skin fade, let us know if you have any skin sensitivities. We recommend arriving 5 minutes early to discuss your desired style with your barber. Our shop is located on 2nd Avenue between 52nd and 53rd Street, with metered street parking available.
How often should I come back to maintain my fade?
For optimal results, we recommend maintaining your fade every 2-3 weeks. Skin fades typically need maintenance every 2 weeks, while regular fades can last 3-4 weeks before needing a touch-up. We offer a loyalty program where every 8th cut is 50% off to help with regular maintenance costs. Book your next appointment before leaving to secure your preferred time slot.
