Frequently Asked Questions About Scissor Hair Cut

How much does a men's haircut cost and what's included?
Our standard men's scissor cut is $45, which includes a thorough consultation, shampoo, precision cut, and styling. For longer styles or more complex cuts, prices may range up to $60. Every service includes a hot towel finish and neck cleanup. We don't use clippers - we're a scissors-only shop specializing in traditional cutting techniques.
Do you take walk-ins or should I make an appointment?
While we do accept walk-ins when possible, we strongly recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (lunch time and after 5pm). You can book online through our website or call us at 212-555-0123. We typically book 2-3 days out during weekdays and up to a week for weekend appointments.
How long does a typical haircut take?
Our standard scissor cuts typically take 45-50 minutes. We believe in taking our time to ensure precision and quality. First-time clients should allow for an hour as we'll spend extra time during the consultation to understand your hair type and style preferences. Please arrive 5-10 minutes before your scheduled appointment time.
I'm looking for a specific style - can you show me examples of your work?
Yes! You can view our portfolio on Instagram @BHBarbersNYC or check out the photo gallery on our website. All our barbers are trained in both classic and contemporary styles, specializing in scissor work. We recommend bringing reference photos to your appointment. Our lead barbers have 10+ years of experience with all hair types and textures.
What's your location and parking situation in the East Village?
We're located at 123 East 7th Street between Avenue A and B, just two blocks from the L train at 1st Avenue. Street parking can be challenging in the East Village - there's metered parking on 7th Street, and a parking garage on Avenue B between 6th and 7th Street. We recommend using public transportation when possible. The M9 bus also stops nearby on Avenue A.
