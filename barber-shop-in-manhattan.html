Frequently Asked Questions About barber shop

Do you take walk-ins or should I book an appointment?
We accept both walk-ins and appointments. However, we strongly recommend booking an appointment, especially during peak hours (weekdays 11am-7pm and weekends). You can book online through our website or call us at (212) 555-0123. Walk-ins are accommodated based on availability, but wait times can be 30-60 minutes during busy periods.
What are your prices for a basic men's haircut?
Our standard men's haircut is $35, which includes a hot towel finish and neck cleanup. For fade haircuts, we charge $40. Senior cuts (65+) are $30. We also offer beard trims for $20 or a full service haircut + beard trim combination for $50. We accept all major credit cards, cash, and digital payments like Venmo and CashApp.
How long does a typical haircut appointment take?
A standard men's haircut typically takes 30-35 minutes. Fade haircuts and more complex styles may take 45 minutes. If you're adding a beard trim, plan for about 45-50 minutes total. We recommend booking a slightly longer slot if it's your first visit, as we'll take extra time to discuss your preferred style.
Are your barbers licensed and experienced with different hair types?
All our barbers are NYS licensed professionals with a minimum of 5 years of experience. Our team is skilled in working with all hair types and textures, including straight, wavy, curly, and coarse hair. Each of our barbers regularly attends continuing education classes to stay current with the latest techniques and trends.
Where is your shop located and what are your parking options?
We're located at 123 East 7th Street between Avenue A and B in the East Village. The closest subway stations are the L train at 1st Avenue (3 blocks away) or the F train at 2nd Avenue (4 blocks). Street parking is available but can be challenging. There's a parking garage on East 6th Street between A and B that offers hourly rates. We're also easily accessible by bus via the M8 and M14A routes.
